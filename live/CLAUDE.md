5. Hour 1: Research + competitive analysis (Intelligence Agent)
6. Hour 2: Brand created (Brand Agent)
7. Hour 3: Website/store live (Website Agent)
8. Hour 4: First outreach sent (Lead Gen or Pipeline Agent)
9. Hour 5: CEO Agent assigned, reporting to Chief of All Chiefs
10. Next morning: first report in briefing

## RULES FOR LIVE OPERATIONS

1. Every new business goes through dispatch_client.launch_new_business()
   Never build infrastructure manually.

2. Every revenue event logged to Airtable immediately.
   RevenueLog table. No exceptions.

3. Opportunity Engine score ≥ 70 → brief Qassim.
   Below 70 → logged but not actioned.

4. All outreach sent AS PluggedIN. Never as a fake persona.
   We are the operator. We find the deals. We make introductions.

5. Every business gets a CEO Agent within Hour 5 of launch.
   No unmanaged businesses in the portfolio.

6. Knowledge Agent updates run before any major campaign.
   Agents know the latest before they act.
