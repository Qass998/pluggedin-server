## THE DELIVERY WORKFLOW (step by step)

Map how a signed client becomes a delivered result.

Step 1 — HANDOVER FROM SALES:
How does Ops receive a new client?
What information comes with them?
What's typically missing that Ops needs to chase?
Who in Ops owns a new client first?

Step 2 — SETUP / ONBOARDING (what Ops does for the client):
What needs to happen before work can start?
Who does it and how long does it take?
What forms, documents, or information are gathered?
What tools or systems need to be set up?

Step 3 — ACTIVE DELIVERY:
Walk through what a typical week looks like for this department.
What tasks repeat daily? Who does them?
What tasks repeat weekly? Who does them?
What tasks repeat monthly? Who does them?
What is tracked and how?

Step 4 — QUALITY CONTROL:
Is there a review or approval step before anything goes to the client?
Who does QC? What do they check?
What does failure at QC look like? What happens then?

Step 5 — CLIENT COMMUNICATION (during delivery):
Who communicates with clients once work has started?
How often do clients hear from the business?
Via what channel? (call / email / WhatsApp / portal)
What do clients typically complain about during delivery?

Step 6 — COMPLETION / CLOSE-OUT:
How is a job marked as complete?
What's the handover to the client at completion?
Is there an invoice trigger? Who sends it?
Is there a review or feedback request?
What happens next — renewal, upsell, or close file?
