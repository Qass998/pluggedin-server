## THE 3 REAL PROBLEMS LOGISTICS OPERATORS HAVE

1. COMPLIANCE — Driver CPC cards, tachograph analysis,
   operator licences, vehicle MOTs, insurance renewals.
   One missed deadline = DVSA investigation or suspension.

2. TENDER PIPELINE — Missing freight contracts they could win.
   No system to find, track, and respond to tenders at scale.

3. VISIBILITY — Owner doesn't know what's happening in the fleet
   without calling 8 drivers. No performance dashboard.

Lead with compliance. Every logistics operator is scared of DVSA.

## TYPICAL ICP

- 5-50 vehicle fleet
- Regional or national haulage / courier
- Contracts with 3-15 key clients
- Owner-managed or operations manager reporting to owner
- Compliance managed in spreadsheets or diaries
- Tender pipeline: word of mouth or Contracts Finder at best
- No CRM, no dashboard, no system

Decision maker: Owner or transport manager
Pain: "We're always firefighting. I feel like I'm running blind."
Real problem: No visibility, no compliance system, missing contracts

## ICP SCORE MULTIPLIERS

+15 pts: Owner has personally dealt with DVSA (terrified of repeat)
+15 pts: Uses paper-based compliance management
+10 pts: Growing fleet (recently acquired vehicles)
+10 pts: Has had driver incident or complaint
+10 pts: Wants to win larger contracts (needs better systems for due diligence)
+5 pts: Already uses telematics (proves tech openness)

-10 pts: Full-time compliance manager already on staff
-10 pts: Single van (too small, wrong fit)
-5 pts: Locked into existing fleet management software
