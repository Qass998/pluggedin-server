## THE 3 REAL PROBLEMS HEALTHCARE OPERATORS HAVE

1. CQC / REGULATORY COMPLIANCE — Reports, care plan reviews,
   medication audits, staff training records. Preparation is manual,
   stressful, and reactive. One inspection without the right
   documentation = serious incident.

2. FAMILY ENQUIRIES — Relatives calling about loved ones.
   After hours, weekends, and holidays. Staff are busy with residents.
   Calls go to voicemail. Families panic. Complaints follow.

3. STAFF ROTA GAPS — Short-notice absences with no backup system.
   Manager scrambles at 6am. Agency costs eat margin.
   Owner gets woken up.

Lead with CQC compliance — it's existential fear territory.

## TYPICAL ICP

Care homes:
- 20-80 beds
- Registered manager + owner (often different people)
- Mix of residential, nursing, or dementia
- CQC rating: Good or Requires Improvement (not Outstanding — they're sorted)
- Monthly revenue: £80,000-500,000
- Paperwork done by managers manually after shifts

Clinics/GP/Physio:
- 2-10 practitioners
- Independent, not NHS bulk contract
- Private pay or mixed
- No-show rate high, re-booking system manual

Decision maker: Owner, registered manager, or GP principal
Pain: "The paperwork never stops. I'm managing, not leading."
Real problem: No system for compliance documentation and family comms

## CQC COMPLIANCE PITCH

"CQC doesn't announce their inspection anymore.
They call the day before at best.

When they arrive, they ask for:
- Evidence of all care plans reviewed in last 12 months
- Medication audit trail for the past 3 months
- Staff training records — every mandatory course
- Safeguarding training dates
- Incident report log with actions taken

Most managers spend 3-4 days before an inspection pulling
this together. Our Data Intelligence module generates
