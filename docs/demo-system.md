## THE DEMO FLOW — MINUTE BY MINUTE

### MINUTE 0-1 — THE HOOK

You say nothing about AI. You say:

"I'm going to show you what your business looks like
when it's running at full capacity. Give me 10 minutes."

Open the dashboard. Full screen. Dark background.
Agents running. Numbers ticking. Briefing on screen.

Let them absorb it for 15 seconds without speaking.

---

### MINUTE 1-3 — THE BRIEFING

Point to the CEO Agent Briefing on screen.

"This is your Chief of Operations.
Every morning at 7am, it reads everything that happened
in your business yesterday — every call, every booking,
every review, every stock level, every competitor move —
and it gives you one briefing. You make decisions.
Everything else runs itself."

Read the sample briefing aloud. It should sound specific,
urgent, and like something they'd actually want to hear.

Sample (restaurant):
"SITUATION: Table bookings down 12% vs last week.
3 one-star reviews mention slow service on Fridays.
Competitor on Church Street dropped lunch prices yesterday.
PRIORITY: Respond to reviews before weekend traffic.
RECOMMENDATION: Push Friday lunch offer via WhatsApp
to loyalty list. 847 customers eligible.
PROCEED? Yes / No / Adjust"

"This one — the Marketing Agent — creates your Instagram

Single-business owners feel pain in one place.

They have a restaurant AND a property portfolio AND a logistics company.

The Empire OS demo doesn't show them one business.

synthesised into one briefing they actually want to read.

The emotional trigger isn't "save time."
It's: "Imagine knowing exactly what's happening in every
business you own, every morning, in under 5 minutes."

That's what you open the demo with.
