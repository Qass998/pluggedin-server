## SCORING DIMENSIONS

Score each firm across 5 dimensions.
Add all points. Maximum score: 100.

### DIMENSION 1 — Practice Area Focus (30 points)

30 points: Primary focus on housing disrepair
           Website dedicated section for housing
           Mentions expert witnesses explicitly

20 points: Mixed personal injury including housing
           Some housing disrepair cases visible

10 points: General personal injury, some housing
           Housing mentioned but not prominent

0 points:  No housing or property law visible
           Commercial only or completely different area

### DIMENSION 2 — Legal Aid and Volume (25 points)

25 points: Legal aid contract confirmed
           AND high volume signals (5+ lawyers)

20 points: Legal aid contract confirmed
           OR high volume signals (5+ lawyers)

10 points: Some legal aid or medium firm (3-5 lawyers)

5 points:  Small firm, no legal aid visible

0 points:  No legal aid, very small or solo practice

### DIMENSION 3 — Buying Signals (25 points)

Score based on signals detected.
Add 5 points per signal found. Maximum 25.

Signals to check (use signal-scanner skill):
→ Hiring housing lawyer or paralegal (+5)
→ Recently posted about housing cases (+5)
→ Reviews mention housing disrepair (+5)
→ Website updated recently (+5)
→ Active on LinkedIn about housing law (+5)
→ News articles about housing wins (+5)

### DIMENSION 4 — Digital Presence (10 points)

10 points: Professional website, active social,
           Google reviews, regular content

7 points:  Good website, some social presence

4 points:  Basic website, minimal online presence

0 points:  Poor website, no online presence

### DIMENSION 5 — Geography (10 points)

10 points: Manchester city centre or Salford
8 points:  Greater Manchester (Stockport, Bolton,
           Oldham, Rochdale, Wigan)
6 points:  Cheshire or Lancashire
4 points:  Yorkshire or West Midlands
0 points:  Outside Northwest England

## TIER THRESHOLDS

TIER 1 (80-100 points): PRIORITY
→ Contact within 24 hours
→ Personalised first email
→ Reference specific signals found
→ Follow up after 3 days if no reply
→ Maximum effort and personalisation

TIER 2 (60-79 points): SECONDARY
→ Contact within 1 week
→ Semi-personalised email
→ Use standard template with customisation
→ Follow up after 5 days

TIER 3 (40-59 points): NURTURE
→ Add to monitoring list
→ Contact when stronger signal detected
→ Do not waste outreach effort yet

TIER 4 (below 40 points): SKIP
→ Not a fit for Gromatic right now
→ Log but do not contact
→ Review in 3 months
