## PLUGGEDIN ICP (Who we sell to)

**Primary target:**
- SMEs with broken or manual growth functions
- 5-50 employees
- Spending £2,000-£10,000/month on sales/marketing manually
- No dedicated BDR or marketing team
- Owner-led businesses where founder does sales themselves
- Industries: Legal, Professional Services, Recruitment, Consulting, Social Care

**Pain points we solve:**
- Spending hours on prospecting with inconsistent results
- Missing leads because no system to find and track them
- Paying £3,000+/month for a BDR who underperforms
- No visibility into pipeline or marketing performance
- Manually writing and sending outreach
- No time to do competitor research or market intelligence

**Buying signals (high intent):**
- Job posting for sales, marketing or BDR role
- LinkedIn posts about growth frustration
- Attending networking events consistently
- Website looks outdated or has no clear CTA
- No social media presence despite being in competitive market
- Recent funding or business expansion news

**Disqualifiers (do not pursue):**
- Less than 5 employees
- Pure e-commerce with no sales function
- Already has a full marketing team
- Less than £500k annual revenue

## GROMATIC ICP (Who Gromatic sells to — our client)

**Target:** Housing disrepair solicitor firms in England and Wales

**Qualifiers:**
- Practices housing disrepair law
- Has a legal aid contract or offers CFAs
- 2-20 fee earners
- Located in major UK cities (Manchester, Birmingham, Leeds, London)
- Currently sourcing cases manually or via referral only

**Buying signals:**
- Job posting for case handler or paralegal
- Website mentions housing disrepair but no clear case funnel
- Active on legal directories (Law Society, Legal 500)
- Low Google review count (opportunity to improve)
- Mentions expert witnesses on website

**Disqualifiers:**
- Personal injury only (no housing disrepair)
- Scottish or Northern Irish firms (different legal system)
- Already partnered with a claims management company
